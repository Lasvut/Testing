Anomaly Detection Testing

About Anomaly Testing

This tool tests the anomaly detection accuracy using:

  • 50 normal traffic samples (70% for training, 30% for testing)
  • 50 malicious traffic samples
  • Calculates Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1-Score, and Specificity
  • Target: ≥80% accuracy for Objective 3
